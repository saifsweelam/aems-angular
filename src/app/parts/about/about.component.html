Who are we?

Abo Elmahasen Sengab Language School (AEMS) are governmental Egyptian school that are co-founded by government funds.

which started our activity in 2003 to provide an international educational service in return for reduced annual fees compared to same kind of schools. . The schools are managed by the Misr Foundation for Educational Administration, and it is supervised by the International AEMS Certificate Unit of the Ministry of Education in partnership with the International Examinations Authority at the British University of Cambridge, which provides technical support to schools and accredits their certificates abroad.

Vision and Mission

AEMS Vision

To play a leading role in transforming teaching & learning culture through providing innovative cutting-edge pedagogical approaches and use of partnerships and technology.

AEMS Mission

To offer a competitive, quality education that prepares cultured, resilient, technologically empowered, global citizens capable of challenging the future for affordable fees.
